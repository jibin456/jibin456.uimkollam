Welcome to UIM KOLLAM

Mission

To provide projects, organizations and people smart and intelligent managers and thought leaders who can work in any team, create, communicate and execute effective value addition strategies and plans, using most modern tools and techniques, assuring the best of results and an increased net worth.

Vision

To create a generation of powerful, value driven individuals who could take humanity to the most glorious of civilizations.

About College

The University Institute of Management Kollam was established in 2006 under the tutelage of the University of Kerala. The Institute is located at Mundakkal in Kollam district of Kerala. The Institute provides Masters in Business Management. It is situated at the technopark building under Kollam Corporation, which is 3 Kms from the Kollam. UIM Kollam is consistently one of the best institute giving unparalleled grounding of university level education in teaching and quality education underthe patronage of Kerala University since 2006. Our College has been in the forefront of quality management education with promising young talents and aim to create an Entrepreneurial attitude, spirit and result oriented motivation amoung budding managers.
