Internships and Comprehensive ViVa

Internship will be considered as a core course in the third semester. The external evaluation will be conducted by the examiners appointed by the University and the internal evaluation by a 3 panel constituted by the college/institute. At the end of the second semester during the summer vacation students will have to go for an internship for a period of 30 days. The objective is to provide an exposure to the students to the real life business situations. The evaluation of the same will be conducted during the start of the third semester. The students will have to submit a report based on their internship and viva voce on the same by external examiners approved by the University will be conducted. The break up for the examination is 50 percent marks for the report evaluation and 50 percent marks for the viva voce examination.

About UIM Kollam

The University Institute of Management Kollam was established under the tutelage of the University of Kerala. The Institute is located at Mundakkal in Kollam district of Kerala. The Institute provides Masters in Business Management. It is situated at the technopark building under Kollam Corporation, which is 3 Kms from the Kollam.
