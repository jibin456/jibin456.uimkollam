ADMISSIONS

PHASE I : ENTRANCE EXAMINATION (WEIGHTAGE 80%)

Attend CAT /CMAT / KMAT KERALA (supervised by Admission Supervisory Committee, Kerala Government).

PHASE II:GROUP DISCUSSION & INTERVIEW CONDUCTED BY THE KERLA UNIVERSITY (WEIGHTAGE20%)

Candidates are evaluated on the basis of their communication, attitude, listening,reasoning ability, leadership and persuasive skills

QUALIFYING MARKS :

Minimum of 50% marks in Part III for BA, B.Sc & B.Com Graduates and 50% aggregate marks (for all semesters/all parts) for all other graduates (48 % in the case of OBC candidate & 35% in the case of SC /ST graduates) is required for Bachelors or Masters degree approved by the University of Kerala to be eligible for MBA course. In the case of candidates awaiting final year/semester results, the confirmation of their admission will be made only after the submission of all the required mark lists.

PROCEDURE FOR ADMISSION:

The candidate desirous of pursuing the MBA (Full-time) Degree programme will have to apply to the institution of his/her choice with a valid CAT/CMAT/KMAT score and also undergo group discussion (GD) and interview at the Institute in order to qualify for admission. There will be a representative of the University in the GD and interview and the list needs to be approved by the University before the student registers for the semester I examination.

About UIM Kollam

The University Institute of Management Kollam was established under the tutelage of the University of Kerala. The Institute is located at Mundakkal in Kollam district of Kerala. The Institute provides Masters in Business Management. It is situated at the technopark building under Kollam Corporation, which is 3 Kms from the Kollam.
