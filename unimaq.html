UNIMAQ 2K18

The University Institute Of Mangement Kollam has organised National Level Management Fest- "UNIMAQ-2K18" which held on 30th NOV and 1st DEC 2018 at MES College Chathanoor.This Fest provided students from different Manangement institutions to exhibit their talentby participating in different Management games- a blend of wide spectrum of formal and informal events.

The aim of this programme is to encourage students participation and to provide them a platform to promote healthy competition and thereby help grooming of budding future managers to specialize in various fields. This fest is an oppurtunity for the young and talented managers to showcase their managerial skills through various events and this is a new beginning for your journey towards endless dreams.

About UIM Kollam

The University Institute of Management Kollam was established under the tutelage of the University of Kerala. The Institute is located at Mundakkal in Kollam district of Kerala. The Institute provides Masters in Business Management. It is situated at the technopark building under Kollam Corporation, which is 3 Kms from the Kollam.
