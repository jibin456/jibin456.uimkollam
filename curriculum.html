CURRICULUM

The MBA (Full-time) Degree programme of the University of Kerala is designed to attract talented young people tobusiness managementand to nurture entrepreneurial capabilities in them in the light of the liberalized and competitive economic and business environment. A deep knowledge of the basics of business administration and management would help them to take up with confidence important assignments in business and also to undertake entrepreneurial projects.

The two year full time MBA program will have four semesters of sixteen weeks each. The first two semesters enhance the fundamental knowledge essential to the understanding of business as well as core knowledge that provides analytical skills for practical application. Participants will sharpen their skills and aptitude thereby strengthening their knowledge in particular areas by opting specializations from the functional domains during the third and fourth semesters.

Electives

1. Financial Management
2. Marketing Management
3. Human Resources Management
4. Systems Management
5. Operations Management

The students opting for dual specializations will have to choose four courses from each elective. Students will also be given the option of choosing all the eight courses from a single elective. Dual specialization certificate to be issued by the University once the candidate applies for the same with a specified fee to the University.

About UIM Kollam

The University Institute of Management Kollam was established under the tutelage of the University of Kerala. The Institute is located at Mundakkal in Kollam district of Kerala. The Institute provides Masters in Business Management. It is situated at the technopark building under Kollam Corporation, which is 3 Kms from the Kollam.
